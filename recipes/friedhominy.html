Fried Hominy

Friend Hominy


Description

A delicious Cherokee staple. There are many variations depending on the area and the family cooking it.

Can be cooked with wild onions and bacon for a more intense, savory dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 strips of good bacon
  • 2 cups of hominy
  • 2 or 3 green onion, or wild onion

Steps

  1. Fry bacon in pan. Cut onion while that is frying.
  2. Crumble bacon and add onions.
  3. When onions seem to be frying, add hominy.
  4. Cook for 10-15 minutes first on high heat, then turn it low after a little while.
