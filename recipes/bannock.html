Bannock Recipe

Bannock


Description

A delicious pan fried bread made with pantry staples, cooked by our ancestors and still widely loved today.

Delicious with honey and jam. Or, add dried berries for extra flavor. This is a great, quick and easy anytime bread.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Stir together all dry ingrediets before adding the wet.
  2. Put dough on kneading surface. Knead about 10 times. Pat into a flat circle about an inch thick.
  3. Warm a greased frying pan on medium heat.
  4. Place dough in pan.
  5. Cook until browned. About 15 min per side.
